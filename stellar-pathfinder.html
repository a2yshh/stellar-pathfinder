BFS
Breadth-First Search
Time: O(b^d) Space: O(b^d) Optimal: Yes* Complete: Yes
BFS explores all neighbors at the current depth before moving deeper — like ripples expanding from a stone dropped in water. Your spacecraft first visits all stars one jump away, then two jumps, etc. Guarantees the shortest path in unweighted graphs. Uses a queue (FIFO).
